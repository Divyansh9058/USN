Escape to Nature: Apple Country Cottages

If you're looking for a feeling of peace in the in the heart of nature, you've found the perfect retreat with the upcoming project The Apple Country by USN Builders & Developers in Almora. A delightful drive along Jaurasi Road leads from the village of Pacharwa to the Apple Country Cottages, which are nestled away from Almora's bustling streets. Thanks to its hilltop location, The Apple Country Cottages offer an ideal stay for nature enthusiasts. Each Apple Country property boasts a gorgeous view and features modern, comfortable furnishings. Its location almost guarantees encounters with breathtaking Almora Valley, rare birds, deer, and peacocks, as well as access to numerous nearby hiking trails.
